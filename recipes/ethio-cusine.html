Doro Wat

A famous Ethipian rich chicken stew made with slow cooked oninons, berbere spice mix, niter kibbeh(spiced butter), garlic and ginger often served with hard boiled egg and injera is a must

Fun facts: most people eat this food on holidays and speacial occasions, and it is not daily consumed food as shiro wat and misr wat (lentil stew)



Ingredients

  • chicken
  • berbere(pepper)
  • butter

Steps of preparation

  1. mix the butter and the pepper
  2. add the chicken
  3. cook it for hrs

kitfo

Kitfo is a traditional Ethiopian dish consisting of finely minced raw beef marinated in a spicy chili powder blend called mitmita and a flavorful herb-infused clarified butter known as niter kibbeh.

Funfact: kitfo is that while it is primarily known for being a raw meat dish, it also comes in varying levels of doneness, from very rare to fully cooked.

Beso

Beso is a traditional Ethiopian and Eritrean staple made from roasted barley flour that can be prepared in various ways, including being mixed with water and spices to form a dough, or as a nutritious drink when mixed with water, honey, or spiced butter.

Anebabero

Anebabero is a layered Ethiopian snack made by stacking two or more cooked pieces of teff injera flatbread, then topping them with a mixture of berbere (Ethiopian chili spice blend) and qibbeh (spiced clarified butter). It's a popular coffee time snack or appetizer that is flavorful, soft, and moist, and can also be made slightly sweet by sprinkling sugar on top.



Ingredients

  • flour
  • spice
  • oil

Steps of preparation

  1. mix the oill and the spice all together
  2. add the flour
  3. mix it with water
  4. bake it

Sega firfir

Sega Firfir is a popular Eritrean and Ethiopian breakfast dish made from shredded injera, a spongy flatbread, mixed with a spiced sauce of meat (sega), berbere, onions, and tomatoes. It's a versatile dish, as "firfir" can refer to the method of shredding and mixing any bread into a sauce, and can be adapted with different proteins like lamb or even vegetables for a vegetarian version
